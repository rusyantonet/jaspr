## Vehicle Mount Computer

The VMC is a vehicle computer that functions as a control and data processing center on the MineLink system, which integrates data from the GPIO Box, sensor units, and communication systems to support real-time monitoring and dispatch in the field.

![VMC1100](images/content/minelink/VMC1100.webp)

![VMC2020](images/content/minelink/VMC_2020_1.jpg)

## GPIO Box

The GPIO Box on MineLink is a hardware interface that functions as a link between the fleet management system and operational signals on heavy equipment units, thus enabling real-time data integration and device control in the field in a reliable and standardized manner.

## GPS Antenna

A GPS antenna is an electronic component specifically designed to receive weak radio signals from Global Positioning System (GPS) satellites in the -band frequency range. Its primary function is to convert these electromagnetic signals into electronic data that is processed by the GPS receiver to determine accurate location, velocity, and time.

## Radio Frequency

A Wireless Radio Client is a radio device that functions as a client/receiver in a wireless network, connected to an Access Point (AP) or primary router. This mode is common in Customer Premises Equipment (CPE).
